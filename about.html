About SurvivorMeter

SurvivorMeter is a light-hearted web experience built to simulate your survival chances in a fictional apocalypse. It mixes humor, personality profiling, and a pinch of nonsense.

  • Purpose: Entertain users with a fun quiz that estimates their survival profile during a hypothetical end-of-the-world scenario.
  • How it works: You answer a few absurd multiple-choice questions. Based on your responses, a "dominant trait" is identified, and a survival time is assigned—no real data or AI modeling involved. Just vibes.
  • Technology: Built using HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No backend. No tracking. No cookies. No nonsense.
  • Creator: A solo indie dev with a passion for design, satire, and making people smile… even during zombie invasions.
  • Philosophy: We believe the internet needs more fun, less fear. SurvivorMeter exists purely to brighten your day with a fictional score and a laugh.

No apocalypse was harmed in the making of this app. All survival predictions are completely fictional and not intended for actual emergency planning (please don’t base your escape strategy on this).
